Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Dot Developer ("we", "our", "us") operates the Infinite Tower Stacker mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

Information We Collect

Username: When you choose to set a username, it is stored on our servers (Supabase) to enable leaderboard features. Usernames are not linked to your real identity.

Game Statistics: We collect gameplay data including scores, combos, blocks placed, zones reached, and games played. This data is associated with your username and used for leaderboard rankings.

Device Information: We do not collect device identifiers. However, third-party services used in the App may collect certain information automatically.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob: Displays advertisements in the App. AdMob may collect device advertising identifiers and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase: Used as our backend database to store usernames, scores, and leaderboard data. See Supabase Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
